Privacy Policy for APEYE

Last Updated: 10/28/24

Introduction

APEYE ("we", "us", or "our") is a Discord bot designed that includes various commands and utilities. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our bot.

Information We Collect

  • User Data: We collect basic information such as your Discord username, user ID, and any other information you provide when you interact with the bot.
  • Messages and Commands: We may log messages and commands you send to the bot to improve functionality and provide support.
  • Server Data: If you invite APEYE to your server, we may collect information about the server such as server ID and member counts.
  • Game Data: We will collect game data like usernames and/or IDs if you choose to connect your account.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our bot's features.
  • To monitor usage and analyze trends to enhance user experience.
  • To communicate with you, including sending updates and responding to inquiries.

Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. You can request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party services that assist us in operating the bot and providing services to you, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccuracies.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of certain communications.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page if you are subscribed. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
